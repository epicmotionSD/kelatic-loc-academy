4-Week Loc Training

Complete week-by-week curriculum breakdown

W1

Foundations & Parting Structure

Learn proper sectioning, grid creation, and the fundamentals of loc architecture. Master the art of clean, even parts that set the foundation for beautiful locs.

W2

Shampooing & Detoxing

Deep dive into scalp health, proper cleansing techniques, and detox methods. Learn product selection and how to prepare hair for optimal loc formation.

W3

Loc Smithing & Styling

Master palm rolling, tension control, and retwisting techniques. Learn protective styles, updos, and how to style locs at various stages of maturity.

W4

Starter Locs & Speed Training

Perfect your starter loc techniques and build speed without sacrificing quality. Time management, workflow optimization, and client consultation skills.

10-Week Loc Master Semester

The most comprehensive loc training available

W1-2

Foundations & Parting Mastery

Deep dive into sectioning theory, grid systems, and precision parting. Work on multiple hair types and textures with live models daily.

W3-4

Shampooing, Detox & Scalp Health

Master cleansing protocols, product chemistry, and scalp treatments. Learn to identify and address common scalp conditions in loc wearers.

W5-6

Advanced Retwisting Techniques

Develop professional-grade retwisting skills with focus on speed, consistency, and tension control. Work with mature locs and various textures.

W7-8

Styling & Creative Techniques

Master updos, braided styles, barrel rolls, and creative loc styling. Learn techniques for weddings, events, and everyday looks.

W9

Crocheting & Interlocking

Learn the art of crochet maintenance and interlocking techniques. Understand when to use each method and perfect your needle work.

W10

Business Development & Certification

Build your brand, set pricing, market your services, and develop client retention strategies. Graduate with your official Loc Academy certification.
